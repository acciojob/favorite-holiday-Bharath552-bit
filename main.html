Bali


Bali is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and lush landscapes.

  1. Uluwatu Temple - A beautiful sea temple.
  2. Kuta Beach - Famous for surfing.
  3. Rice Terraces - Stunning views of rice paddies.
  4. Monkey Forest - Home to playful monkeys.
  5. Seminyak - Known for its upscale shopping and dining.
  • Nasi Goreng - Fried rice with vegetables and meat.
  • Satay - Grilled meat skewers served with peanut sauce.
  • Gado-Gado - Mixed vegetables with peanut sauce.
  • Rendang - Spicy beef stew.
  • Babi Guling - Suckling pig, a Balinese delicacy.
